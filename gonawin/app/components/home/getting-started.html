1. Getting started

Getting started

By using gonawin, you will be able to play with your friends around your favorite sport events.

Sign in



Join us by clicking on the sign in button in the home page and choose to log in with either your Google+, Google, Twitter or Facebook account.

Start

Once you sign up you will have access to the following:

  • You will be able to either join an existing team, or create your own (teams can be private or public) by clicking in the Teams menu.
  • You can also set the tournaments available for you to play on the Tournaments menu.
  • In the user drop down menu you will find all the information relative to the user.

Teams

Your team administrator will be able to sign your team up to an existing tournament and all the team members will be able to start playing said tournament right away.
You can be part of as many teams as you wish.

User information

Profile

In your profile page you will find the following:

  • The teams you have joined
  • The tournaments you are participating in
  • Your pending team requests
  • Your pending team invites

Settings

In the settings section, you will find your profile information.

Invite

In the invite section you can send invitations to your friends to join you on gonawin.

How to play

On every match there is a gonawin button. By clicking on it you will see a form that you can fill in with your guess. Then just hit save and your prediction will be recorded.

Tournaments

Right now the only tournament supported is the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
In the tournament page you will find the different matches, the groups and the ranking of teams and users in this tournament.

If you guessed correctly the score of a match you will get 3 points. If you guess correctly the trend of the match (meaning a draw, or the winner) you will get 1 point.
