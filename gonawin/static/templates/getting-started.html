Getting started

Using gonawin you will be able to play with your friends on sport events.

Sign in

Join us by clicking on the sign in button in the home page and choose to log in with either your Google+, Google, Twitter or Facebook account.

Start

Once you sign up you can either join an existing team, or create your own (teams can be private or public). Your team administrator will be able to sign your team up to an existing tournament and all the team members will be able to start playing said tournament right away.
You can be part of as many teams as you wish.

Profile

In your profile page you will find the following:

  • The teams you have joined
  • The tournaments you are participating in
  • Your pending team requests
  • Your pending team invites

In the settings section, you will find your profile information.
In the invite section you can send invitations to your friends to join you on gonawin.

How to play

On every match there is a gonawin button. By clicking on it you will see a form that you can fill in with your guess. Then just hit save and your prediction will be recorded.

Tournaments

Right now the only tournament supported is the Fifa World Cup 2014.
In the tournament page you will find the different matches, the groups and the ranking of teams and users in this tournament.

If you guessed correctly the score of a match you will get 3 points. If you guess correctly not the score but the winner you get 1 point.
