About gonawin:

A social web application to make "friendly bets" on sport games with your friends.

We believe that today's betting platforms are full of ads that polute the game itself. We want to create a platform that people can use to bet/play with their friends and family or anyone they want, without ads in between. There is no money involved.

Contributors : Santiago, Remy, Cabernal

Full stack:

Here is the development stack:

  • Google App Engine for Go
  • Boostrap v3
  • Angularjs
  • go-oauth
  • Flags
  • Avatars
  • Social Buttons for Bootstrap
  • Icons
    • font-awesome
    • glyphicons
